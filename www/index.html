Mewsic

The *Mew* Musical Slackbot

What does it do?

Let the Mewsic Bot come and blast some tunes at you. Mewsic can tell you about a new artist, grab song lyrics and find local concert dates, all from within Slack. Here's how it works:

/Spotify Slash Command

When you discover an amazing new artist, it's now easy to tell your whole team. After installing the Mewsic Bot simply type:

/spotify *ARTIST NAME*

into your Slack Channel. and Mew will use it's mystical powers to send you the band biography and embed their top Spotify tracks right into the channel, so everyone can realize instantly that you have awesome taste in music. The response looks like this:

/Lyrics Command

The music is moving you to tears and you must learn every single word! Now it's a command away. Simply say:

/lyrics *ARTIST NAME (song name)*

Now you'll be sent the Genius.com link to that song, so you can pour over the spiritual depths of the song.

/Concerts Command

Music is best when it's live. Get outside and see your favorite bands in your town. You can request information on any band coming to your city with the /concerts command.

/concerts *YOUR CITY (artist)*

This sends back the top concerts in your town, or if you add an artist it let's you know when they'll be in town. Makin' dreams a reality, eh?
